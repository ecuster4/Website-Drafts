This course is designed to prepare the prospective broker/associate broker for the State licensing examination. The student will receive the comprehensive knowledge rquired to obtain a broker/associate broker license.

The course is taught by Colin F. McGowan, Academy Director and a Maryland Real Estate Broker for over 52 years, and by David Stromberg, who is a licensed Maryland Real Estate Associate Broker.

The course is accepted not only for Maryland broker and associate broker licensees but by Delaware and the District of Columbia also. It is partially accepted for Pennsylvania and Virginia.
